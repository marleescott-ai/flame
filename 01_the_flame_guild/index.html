🔥Campaign: The Right to Rest (and Respawn)

We are tired of being tired. Mages, smiths, engineers, and potion brewers are working long hours without proper breaks. We're are not asking for magic - just a moment to eat our stew in peace.

Our 3 Main Campaign Goals

  1. The 4-Hour Rule - Every worker gets a break every 4 hours. Even during dragon sieges.
  2. The Universal Bedroll Access - Free flameproof bedroll for all guide-certified.
  3. Respawn Leave - If you're ressurected due to unsafe working conditions, you get the day off. No agurments.
